Frequently asked questions

What size should I order?

We recommend referring to our size guide for accurate measurements. You can also use our virtual fitting tool for personalized recommendations based on your foot measurements..

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free shipping on all orders within the continental United States. For international orders, shipping rates may apply.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to track your order on our website or through the shipping carrier's website.

Feel free to contact us and we'll help you out as soon as we can.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy.

Find out more information by reading the license.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods. You can choose your preferred payment option during checkout.

Are your shoes suitable for people with foot conditions like plantar fasciitis?

We have shoes with features such as arch support, cushioning, and stability that may be suitable for individuals with foot conditions like plantar fasciitis. We recommend consulting with a healthcare professional for personalized recommendations.

Are your shoes suitable for specific activities like running or hiking?

Yes, we have a range of shoes designed for various activities, including running, hiking, walking, and more. You can use our filters to narrow down your search based on your activity needs.

How can I contact customer support for assistance?

You can reach our customer support team via email, phone, or live chat on our website. We're here to help with any questions or concerns you may have.
